Math Department

Sullivan Community College

Sullivan Community College's Math Department is very proud of its Honors Program. First year students are invited to join the Honors Program based on past transcripts and test scores. Other students are welcome to apply for admission to the program. Applicants must have a department interview, write an essay, and take an assessment exam. Students may apply at any point in their years at Sullivan.

Membership in the Honors Program allows you to take Honors level classes and to be eligible for scholarships, internships, and teaching assistant positions.
