Baking FAQs

Why do Baking?
Because it's a fun thing to do and at the end you get to eat what you created!


What if i don't know how to bake/cook?
You don't need to be an expert baker or cook to bake. There are multiple books & videos where you can learn how to bake.


But i don't have a big kitchen to bake things.
It doesn't matter if you have a big or small kitchen you can still bake things whether their small or big. Just have fun!


I am new to baking. What are the three must have baking utensiles?
1) A great microwave-safe-bowl
2) A balloon whisk
3) A good set of scales
