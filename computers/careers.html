Careers in Computer Repair Services

Training

If you're considering a career in computer repair, check your local community or career college for courses. You should be knowledgeable about various operating systems and software, and have hands-on experience troubleshooting peripheral devices such as printers.

Certification

CompTIA's certification is one of the most common industry certifications. To obtain certification, take a course or purchase self-study materials at your local bookstore.
