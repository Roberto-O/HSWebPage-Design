Keeping The City Clean and Safe

 

If I were to be elected as State Governor would always make sure to keep the city as clean and as safe as possible. I would always have people making sure that there is no trash on the ground near our parks and our schools. Also if anyone is caught littering action will be taken. I willl also try to completely remove any illegal groups from our city.

Another thing i will do is listen to the towns people (you) to try to satisfy you as much as i can. If you have any concerns or wants something added to our city, like a new park, playground, school, or building i can make it happen.
