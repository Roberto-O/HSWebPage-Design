Baskets by Carole

Company History

Nearly 80 years ago, Baskets by Carole began with a single product and a single employee. Today, Baskets by Carole, located in Tampa, Florida, is operated by three generations of family members.

The company is known for its exceptional quality and service. The company produces a wide variety of baskets, including styles for holidays and special occasions. Our energies are focused on customers rather than competitors. We believe in the "old fashioned" idea that serving customers is the reason we are in business.

We honor our deadlines and strive for quality and memborable service.
